# ADHD Workplace Toolkit

An open-source toolkit of practical resources for adults with ADHD in the professional environment.

## Mission Statement

Our mission is to create a collaborative, evidence-based repository of strategies, templates, and guides that empower adults with ADHD to thrive in their careers. We aim to reduce stigma, increase accessibility, and provide actionable tools that can be adapted to diverse professional contexts.

## Contribution Guidelines

We welcome contributions from professionals, advocates, and individuals with lived experience. To contribute:

1. **Fork the repository** and create a new branch for your work.
2. **Review existing issues** or open a new issue to discuss your proposed addition.
3. **Follow the established structure** (see below) and maintain a respectful, inclusive tone.
4. **Submit a pull request** with a clear description of your changes.
5. **Engage in the review process** – be open to feedback and suggestions.

All content should be:
- Practical and actionable
- Respectful of neurodiversity
- Free of medical advice (this toolkit is for informational purposes only)
- Licensed under [CC BY-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/)
